Mac Install — SlideFlipper

Turn your watch into a clicker 🖱

Download the app — it runs silently in your menu bar, no Terminal needed.

1
Choose the correct Mac download below.
2
Open the downloaded .dmg file and drag SlideFlipper into your Applications folder.
3
If it opens normally, a small icon appears in your menu bar.
4
To avoid Mac warnings, download and run the helper below after installing.
Download for Apple Silicon Mac Download for Intel Mac

macOS 11 or later  ·  Apple Silicon: M1 / M2 / M3 / M4  ·  Intel: older Macs

⚠️ First time opening? macOS may warn "unverified developer" — that's normal for unsigned apps. Right-click the app → Open → Open to run it once. After that it opens normally.

Recommended

After installing the DMG, download this helper and double-click it.

Download .command Helper

You can delete the `.command` file after it finishes running.

or

If you prefer Terminal, copy and run this command once:

xattr -cr /Applications/SlideFlipper.app

After opening SlideFlipper:

  • A small icon appears in your menu bar (top-right of screen).
  • Click it to see your IP address.
  • Enter that IP in the watch's IP SETUP screen.
  • Open your presentation and tap FLIPPER on the watch. You're live!
